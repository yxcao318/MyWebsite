在家的一百次意外死亡  100 Ways of Losing Lives at Home  (2019，进行中)


产品定位 / Product Information

         科普家庭用火用电安全的黑色幽默解谜手游
         A black humored mobile puzzle game about home safety

使用软件 / Made With

         Figma, Photoshop, Unity

游戏背景及玩法 / Game Setting and Mechanics

         随着剧情展开，玩家需要在家中完成一系列任务，涉及厨房安全、家用电器安全等方面（比如使用微波炉加热鸡蛋）， 如果玩家出现任何有安全隐患的操作，游戏会提示玩家“死亡”一次，直至玩家按照正确步骤完成任务，剧情才会继续推进。 游戏玩法与密室逃脱类游戏相似，在屋内寻找可用道具（比如鸡蛋、刀具等），相互配合，解决谜题。
         Players would complete a series of missions at home about kitchen safety and usage of electronic appliances (such as microwaving an egg). They would “die” once and again if missions are completed with potential dangers until they get the right steps. The gameplay is similar to room escape games in which players look for things (such as eggs and knives) in the room and combine them to solve puzzles.

其他功能 / Other Features

         1. 收集成功完成的任务和不同死亡方式的徽章
         1. Collect survival and death emblems
         2. 查看家庭安全百科
         2. Look through tips for home safety
