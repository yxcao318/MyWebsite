白日梦  Daydream  (2020)


产品定位 / Product Information

         探讨性别成见问题的互动叙事游戏演示
         An interactive narrative game demo targeting gender stereotypes of women

用户定位 / Target Audience

         全年龄用户群
         All ages

使用软件 / Made With

         橙光制作工具
         Chengguang Game Maker

游戏背景及玩法 / Game Setting and Mechanics

         玩家扮演一位事业有成的男士，有一位漂亮能干的新婚妻子，两人在一起度过的每一天都会发生不同的事情，玩家需要通过选择代表自己想法的选项与妻子及外部环境进行互动。
         In the game players will play as a husband, newly married to a lovely and capable wife, and lead a life together with her. They will read through conversations and make dilemmatic choices to interact with the wife and the environments by clicking on the mouse button.

游戏目标及结局 / Game Goal and Finale

         玩家需要维持妻子和自身的心情值及体力值，如果有维度达到零则游戏结束，妻子会告诉玩家她只是玩家的一个梦，谢谢玩家一直以来替她着想、希望她快乐的所作所为。 玩家醒来后发现屋里的一切都还是个人生活时的样子，但餐桌上摆放着准备好的早餐。
         The goal is to maintain the mood and energy values of both the husband and the wife so that the days will go on and more scenarios will show up, otherwise the game ends when any value decreases to zero. Then the wife will tell the husband that she is just a dream of his, and that she wishes all the difficulties she is facing could be conquered by his love. Finally she will disappear when he wakes up, but his breakfast will be put on the dining table as a comforting finale.

游戏主旨 / Game Theme

         从旧时代的“三从四德”到新时代的“独立自主”都是社会成见为女性贴下的标签，游戏希望玩家意识到性别成见给女性(以及男性)带来的压力有复杂的社会因素，并非是身边人出于好意的关心和举动就能解决的。 比如玩家如果希望尊重妻子的穿衣自由，那就要考虑到她独自外出的安全问题，甚至还有网络媒体带来的压力，在帮她挑选合适的防身用品时也会陷入新的困境…… 游戏通过种种两难的选择引发玩家的深思——性别问题不是改变某一个个体的举动，更重要的是打破社会成见的固化思维。
         The game is to leave some food for thought that the deep influence brought by the stereotypes of women is not easy to eliminate as a social issue even if players think of everything from the wife’s perspective, let alone simply encourage her to follow her heart regardless of social reality. The generalized and fossilized social expectations need to be broken.

产品特点 / Product Features

         1. 真实的生活场景(妻子工作出众却感到疲惫、需要独自出席晚宴等等)
         1. Simulated real-life scenarios (e.g. the wife asking for advice on what to wear for a late-night party, the couple discussing whether the wife should accept the promotion at work)
         2. 文字选项、图片选项、手机对话框、新闻评论等不同互动形式
         2. Different interaction modes (e.g. image choices, mobile chat box, news comments)
         3. 打破“理所当然”的两难选项
         3. Dilemmatic choices that confront “taking it for granted”
         4. 超现实的温馨结局
         4. A surreal comforting finale
