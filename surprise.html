意外  Surprise  (2020)


活动介绍 / Developing Context

         所属实验室1.5小时游戏开发挑战，主题为“Surprise”
         1.5-hour game jam entitled “Surprise” organized by the Games Research Lab of my college

游戏定位 / Game Information

         展现了充满隐含意的四个“意外”的第一人称体验游戏
         A first-person experiential game with four “surprises” and connotations embedded

使用软件 / Made With

         Unity

游戏理念 / Game Concept

         1. 你正在爬一个直入天际的长坡，落石滚滚而来，你不小心与它们相撞，却没有跌倒。它们暗喻着拦在你和目标之间的困难。
         1. The big meteors coming towards you are fake. They resemble the distractions in your life that keep us away from your goals.
         2. 你正为落石的幻象所迷惑，却不知在何处跌落下了长坡，进入黑蒙蒙的水底。
         2. While you are fighting illusions, your vision becomes blurry so that you could not see other traps and fall into the dark sea.
         3. 你找到了水中的亮光，回归正轨，又一个落石滚来，这次它是真的，把你撞回了出发点。
         3. You walk towards the light spot underwater that brings you back onto your way. But a real meteor knocks you down to the start.
         4. 你从头继续爬坡，走了很久之后，天上垂下一根发光的绳索，将你直接拉了上去。你从来都没被遗忘。
         4. You go again from the start, and after a long while a luminous rope lifts you up to the sky. You are never forgotten.
