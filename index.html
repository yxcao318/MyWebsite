I am a passionate gamer, serious game designer and researcher. With a Bachelor's degree in English Language and Literature from Fudan University, I am currently a Master candidate in Design and Development of Digital Games at Columbia University. I am mostly interested in addressing social and cultural issues with games, and expect to explore the integration of interactive narrative into game mechanics.

作品集 / Portfolio


此作品集包含我本人独立制作及与他人共同制作的游戏、手机应用、手绘作品等，均为课程作业和学术研究项目，语言为英语，部分使用网络开放资源，请勿以任何形式抄袭或盗用。


This portfolio is a collection of my game design, application design and drawing works. All of them are course and research projects in the English Language, some of which use open assets online. Plagiarism in all forms PROHIBITED.

音嬉 / Musices

“音嬉”是一款作曲教学手机应用，提供和弦拼接、乐器演奏、五线谱和midi乐谱三种作曲方式，同时还提供乐队功能，使用户可以协作演奏或作曲。

Musices is a mobile application for music composition, providing three ways of composing with chord splice, musical instruments, score and midi notations. Band function enables users to work collaboratively in composing.

在家的一百次意外死亡 / 100 Ways of Losing Lives at Home

“在家的一百次意外死亡”是一款关于生活安全的手机游戏，通过各种意外死亡的黑色幽默提醒玩家防范用火用电等家庭安全隐患。（Unity可运行版本尚在制作中）

The mobile game 100 Ways of Losing Lives at Home expects to raise the awareness of home safety in a tone of black humor. (Unity demo in progress)

回绿 / H&MxGreen

“回绿”是以H&M公司为利益相关者，为宣传其环保品牌所做的手机应用，旨在为旧衣回收提供便利，提升用户的环境意识。

H&MxGreen is a mobile application to boost users’ consumption of H&M’s Conscious clothing line and encourage users to recycle old clothes and develop environmental awareness.

九重天 / Ninth World

“九重天”是基于rpgmaker游戏引擎制作的一个迷你叙事游戏，旨在探讨父母对于子女玩游戏的不理解和子女过度沉迷游戏的问题。

Ninth World is a mini role-playing game made with rpgmaker, which aims to address the misunderstanding of parents about games and children’s addiction to games.
