回绿  H&MxGreen


产品定位 / Product Information

H&M品牌环保系列专属手机应用
An application for H&M Conscious Exclusive line and clothes recycling

用户定位 / Target Audience

经常购买快时尚衣物及无处处理旧衣的人群
Fast-fashion consumers and those who seek a way to recycle old clothes

使用软件 / Made With

Photoshop

产品功能 / Product Features

1. 学习难度选择及个人空间管理
1. Select learning level and monitor personal progress

2. 使用和弦拼接进行创作，加入不同器乐声部，选用节奏型
2. Compose with chord splice, set beats and modes

3. 使用各种乐器、五线谱或midi乐谱进行创作
3. Compose with musical instruments and score notations

4. 完成教程解锁不同音乐流派风格，在创作中用作模板
4. Unlock music styles as composition resources after completing tutorials

5. 组成乐队，基于前一位创作者的片段继续创作，合作演奏
5. Compose according to others' music pieces and play as a band
