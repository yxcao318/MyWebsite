回绿  H&MxGreen  (2017)


产品定位 / Product Information

H&M品牌环保系列专属手机应用
An application for H&M Conscious Exclusive line and clothes recycling

用户定位 / Target Audience

经常购买H&M快时尚衣物且无处处理旧衣的人群
Fast-fashion consumers of H&M clothes who seek a way to recycle old clothes

使用软件 / Made With

Photoshop

产品功能 / Product Features

1. 关注H&M环保系列上新，应用内购买环保系列衣物
1. Keep updated with H&M Conscious Exclusive line, and purchase clothes within the application

2. 扫描需要回收的H&M衣物二位码，与数据库进行匹配，等待取件
2. Scan the QR code for the H&M clothes to be recycled, match them with the database for parcel collection

3. 完成回收，获得优惠码、电子衣物等奖励，用环保服饰装扮角色，尝试搭配效果
3. Get coupons and e-clothes for recycling rewards, and try Conscious Exclusive clothes on your avatar
