3D拼图游戏  3D Puzzle Game  (2020)


产品定位 / Product Information

         提升少儿空间推理能力的3D拼图游戏
         A 3D puzzle game demo to improve the spatial reasoning ability of young kids

用户定位 / Target Audience

         6-12岁学龄儿童
         Aged 6-12

使用软件 / Made With

         Unity

游戏目标及玩法 / Game Goal and Mechanics

         玩家通过旋转查看小窗中的目标模型，在界面中点击按钮转换视角，在三视图中拖拽拼接立体图形，使其与小窗中的模型一致。
         In the game players could switch to three different perspectives from which blocks of different shapes could be dragged and spliced to consist of the models in the upper-left window.

产品特点 / Product Features

         1. 操控视角，感受二维和三维世界的转换
         1. Play with 2D and 3D perspectives
         2. 淡雅配色，保护视力
         2. Mild colors for eye protection
