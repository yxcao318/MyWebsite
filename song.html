梦游宋都  Wandering in Song  (2020，进行中)


产品定位 / Product Information

         可演奏中国古曲、游览古风建筑景观的音乐跑酷手机游戏
         A music parkour mobile game demo that enables players to play Chinese ancient songs and view cultural landscape

使用软件 / Made With

         Unreal Engine 4

游戏目标及玩法 / Game Goal and Mechanics

         玩家通过左右划动手机屏幕在跑道上移动角色，上划跳跃，下划后仰，躲避随机生成的障碍物，碰撞收集“宫商角徵羽”音符，连成中国古曲的旋律。
         Players would move the character by swiping left and right on the mobile screen, swiping up to jump and down to lean back to avoid random obstacles while collecting Chinese music notes to consist of ancient songs.

产品特点 / Product Features

         1. 连击音符越多，音乐呈现越复杂，从单音到和弦再到多乐器组合，同时体验跑酷与奏乐
         1. The more combos of music notes you get, the more complicated the music presentations will be (from single note to chords to multi-instruments)
         2. 碰到障碍物后，游戏中会弹出文物相关的小问题，答对后可收集此文物
         2. Answer questions about Chinese cultural relics that pop up when the character runs into obstacles to collect the relics
         3. 通过单局得分积累铜钱和元宝解锁不同的古曲曲目及跑酷场景
         3. Unlock more ancient Chinese songs and parkour scenes with the silver and gold gained from each level
